Uninstalling Spatial Workstation on macOS¶

The Spatial Workstation will need to be uninstalled by manually removing:

  1. The folder in Mac HD/Applications/FB360 Spatial Workstation
  2. The AAX plugins (FB360-Spatialiser and FB360-Control) in Mac HD/Library/Application Support/Avid/Audio/Plugin-Ins
  3. The VST plugins ​(FB360-Spatialiser and FB360-Control) in Mac HD/Library/Audio/Plugin-Ins​/VST and Mac HD/Library/Audio/Plugin-Ins​/VST​3
  4. /usr/local/lib/libFB360-PluginShell.dylib
