Can I use Logic Pro/X with the Spatial Workstation?¶

The Spatial Workstation plugins are currently available only as VSTs and AAX. AU plugin support for Logic Pro/X is currently unavailable.

I have completed my mix, what do I do with it?¶

The FB360 Encoder can be used to encode or convert your mix into a range of formats.

Facebook 360

The Encoder can be used to a create a mp4 with spatial audio for Facebook 360

Creating Videos With Spatial Audio For Facebook 360

Oculus Video (Gear VR)

Listen to your mix in Oculus Video (Gear VR) by either side-loading or streaming.

Add Spatial Audio To Oculus Video (Gear VR)

Oculus Video (Rift)

Listen to your mix in Oculus Video on the Oculus Rift by side-loading.

Add Spatial Audio To Oculus Video (Oculus Rift)

Ambisonics

The FB360 Encoder can be used convert your mix to B-format (either FuMa or ambiX). This audio can then be used to deploy your videos on YouTube.

Also see:

Add Spatial Audio For YouTube

Quad-Binaural

Quad-binaural is a very simple format supported by some apps and platforms.

Also see:

Encoding a Quad-Binaural Mix

Rendering Engine

If you are developing your own VR or 360 video app, the FB360 Rendering Engine SDK can be used to playback a .tbe file in sync with a video.

How Do I Test My Mix?¶

The FB360 Encoder can be used to encode or convert your mix into multiple formats:

Oculus Video (Gear VR)

You can preview encodings for Facebook 360 using Oculus Video on Gear VR by either side-loading or streaming.

Add Spatial Audio To Oculus Video (Gear VR)

Oculus Video (Oculus Rift)

You can preview encodings for Facebook 360 using Oculus Video on the Rift by side-loading.

Add Spatial Audio To Oculus Video (Oculus Rift)

.tbe

.tbe files can be tested with a video by using the desktop FB360 Video Player in Standalone mode.

B-format (ambiX)

B-format audio can be tested using the JUMP Inspector (Android).

Add Spatial Audio For YouTube

Quad Binaural

Quad-binaural output can be tested with Samsung VR.

Encoding a Quad-Binaural Mix

Why can’t I use the non-HD version of Pro Tools?¶

The Spatial Workstation requires a minimum of 9 channel buses, which only the HD version of Pro Tools supports.

What DAWs support the Spatial Workstation?¶

The Spatial Workstation is currently tested and supported with the following DAWs on OSX:

  1. Pro Tools HD
  2. Reaper
  3. Nuendo

What About Ableton Live?¶

The Spatial Workstation requires a minimum of 9 channel buses. Ableton Live only supports 2 channel buses.
